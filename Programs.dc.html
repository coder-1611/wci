A clear pathway from your first free tryout to national tournaments — with group training, 1:1s, strength & conditioning, and a packed calendar of league and championship games.

The pathway

How you join & grow at WCI

1
Free tryout

Register for a free Basic Membership and attend a tryout session. Meet the coaches, get assessed, and feel the environment — no commitment.

2
Become a Premium Member

Join as a Premium Member to unlock coaching & games. We build your Individual Development Plan (IDP) across every discipline.

3
Train & compete

Register for Group Training & Games, then play in local leagues and national tournaments with the Austin Blasters.

Good to know

Sessions, gear & grounds

→

WCI provides balls, stumps & drill equipment. Players bring their own bat & protective gear, and wear the academy jersey & sports shoes.

→

Sessions run at Round Rock Cricket Ground (RRCG) & Williamson County (Wilco) grounds, following City & County field schedules.

→

Seasonal Summer & Spring Break camps run throughout the year — ask us for the latest dates.
