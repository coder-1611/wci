The WCI Difference

Where playing to win becomes a habit

Founded in 2021 by Level-4 Elite Coach Alok Singh, WCI Academy turns young cricket enthusiasts into high-performance talents. Players don't just sharpen skills and temperament — they build a winning mindset, leadership, and confidence on and off the field.

We're a registered youth academy of USA Cricket, recognized by the ICC and USOC, and we leverage AI-driven CoachED tools to evaluate every player and personalize their development plan.

01
Elite, world-class coaching
Mentored by Gary Kirsten Cricket. Coaches certified Level-2 to Level-4 Elite through CoachED.
02
AI-driven development
Every player gets an Individual Development Plan with data-backed drills from CoachED's AI tools.
03
A real path to compete
Train, then play in local leagues, USA Cricket Hubs, and national tournaments with Austin Blasters.
