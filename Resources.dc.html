Resources & player pathway

How we develop young cricketers the right way — the Long-Term Athlete Development model, plus everything parents need to know before the first session.

Our framework

Long-Term Athlete Development

We coach to a structured LTAD model — the framework developed by internationally renowned coach Istvan Balyi and rooted in the major sports of the U.S.

Gary Kirsten Cricket adapts these principles to integrate training, competition and recovery with each child's biological development — for a fulfilling journey in and through the game.

01Ages ~0–6

Active Start

Fun, daily physical activity. Learning fundamental movements and linking them into play.

02G 6–8 · B 6–9

FUNdamentals

Well-structured, fun activities 3–4× a week. Building the ABCs of movement — no formal competition.

03G 8–11 · B 9–12

Learn to Train

"Hard-ball" cricket begins. Developing batting, bowling, fielding & keeping movement skills.

04Teens

Train to Train

Skill + physical development. Learning how to practice, with care for shoulders, core, spine & ankle stability.

05Teens

Train to Compete

Constant improvement of cricket-specific skills, game awareness and the mental side of the game.

06Elite

Train to Win

Identified elite athletes pursue cricket as a career through intensive, high-performance programs.

07

Active for Life

A lifelong love of the game — players can enter this stage at any age after building physical literacy.

Detailed goals & activities for boys and girls are available on request — contact@wcicricket.com

Questions

Frequently asked

How do we get started?+
Register for a free Basic Membership and attend a tryout session. After that, become a Premium Member ($120/year) to unlock coaching and games, then register for Group Training & Games. Separate registration is required for Premium and for Group Training & Games.
What does it cost?+
Premium Membership is $120/year (non-refundable). Group Training & Games is a monthly subscription — $300/month for 12 sessions (roughly 2 group trainings + 1 game per week), and ranges $299–$399/month depending on sessions per week. Prefer to drop in? Around $30 per training session and $30 per game.
Can I register more than one child?+
Yes. After you register one player, the Review page offers "Register Another Person," which takes you back to the start to register a second (or more) child.
What about make-ups & cancellations?+
There are no make-up sessions for missed classes. Subscriptions can be cancelled on a 30-day notice, effective after the next monthly payment cycle. The $120 annual membership fee is non-refundable.
Where can I get the detailed LTAD plan?+
A detailed version with recommended goals and activities for both boys and girls is available from WCI Academy. Email contact@wcicricket.com or WhatsApp 512-200-3042 and we'll share it.
