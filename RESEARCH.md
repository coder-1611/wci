# WCI Academy — Research Dossier (for redesign)

Source: wcicricket.com + USA Cricket / SportsEngine / press. Compiled to keep facts accurate.

## What WCI is
- **WCI Academy** — premier **youth cricket academy for high-performance**, headquartered in **Austin, TX**.
- **Founded 2021 by Alok Singh** (Level-4 Elite Coach, CoachED by Gary Kirsten; corporate executive; USA Cricket Volunteer Youth Coordinator for Central Texas since 2020).
- **Registered youth academy of USA Cricket** (national governing body, recognized by ICC and USOC).
- **Powered by Gary Kirsten Cricket (GKC) & CoachED.** Gary Kirsten = World-Cup & IPL-winning coach who **mentors/guides** (NOT the day-to-day head coach — important per client).
- Coaches certified by CoachED programs **Level-2 to Level-4 Elite**. Uses **CoachED AI tool** for drill recommendations + player evaluation.
- Tagline: **"Youth Cricket Academy for High-Performance."**
- Nonprofit arm: **Cricket4All.org** (opportunities for players from diverse backgrounds).
- **Sept 2025: strategic partnership with Super Kings Academy (SKA)** — official youth academy of IPL's Chennai Super Kings (CSK) — established a **High-Performance Cricket Center** in Austin (indoor facility + premium outdoor fields at Williamson County Park, North Austin).

## Programs & structure
- **Membership funnel:** (1) Free "Basic Membership" → attend a **tryout session**; (2) become **Premium Member**; (3) register for **Group Training & Games** (separate registration + fees).
- **Premium Membership:** $120 / 12 months (~$10/mo), non-refundable.
- **Group Training:** ~$300/month for 12 sessions (monthly subscription).
- Develops an **Individual Development Plan (IDP)** per player. Trains S&C + batting/bowling/fielding/wicket-keeping, group & 1:1.
- Equipment: WCI provides balls/stumps/drill gear; players bring own bat + protective gear + academy jersey + sports shoes.
- Grounds: **Round Rock Cricket Ground (RRCG)** and **Williamson County (Wilco) ground**; astro-turf fields across Greater Austin.

## Leagues & tournaments (where they compete)
- **AYL (Austin Youth League):** U9 (Fri), U11 (Sat), U13/15 (Sun). Divisions incl. Cubs (U9), Junior.
- **USA Cricket Hub Games:** U11/13/15, Austin → Zonal → National.
- **LPCL (Lone Star Premier League), CTCL (Central Texas Cricket League):** U15/17/19 / adult leagues.
- **AMLCA (aka MLC Jr):** U11/13/15/17 on Memorial Day, Summer Derby, Thanksgiving.
- **National tournaments:** DYCL & NTCA (Dallas), NYCT & TYPL (Houston), AMLCA Elite Championship.
- **Professional:** Houston Premier League (HPL), Natural Turf (PVCC), Houston.
- **Austin Spring Championship (ASC)** — WCI's own multi-age tournament (ASC 2024, ASC 2026). 4 astro-turf fields; live-streamed games; per-game awards (Best Fielder/Bowler/Batter, Player of the Match).
- Track record: "the only cricket academy in Austin with consistent tournament wins across age-groups"; Austin Blasters has **won multiple championships at national levels**.
